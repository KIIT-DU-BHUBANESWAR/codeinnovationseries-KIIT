Emergency

In this Emergency section, the patient will choose the specified medical field and as they choose any tab, immediately an alert is sent to the Doctor assigned for the emergency. If the assigned Doctor doesn't respond to the alert within 2 minutes, then the site automatically assigns the next doctor on duty for an emergency. This process continues till a doctor accepts the alert.
