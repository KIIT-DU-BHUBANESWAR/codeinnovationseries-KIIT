ABOUT US

As college students, we spent years tutoring and leading successful study groups. But we noticed a ton of students struggling with the same thing every semester: study prep. Our website was developed to address a growing problem in the minds of the Students of the 21st century: How and where to get the notes for their upcoming examinations? It is very clear theses days that effective note taking and study prep were two of the greatest obstacles to success for students.

And since every problem comes with a solution, we hereby welcome you all brilliant minds to come and experience a galore of learning with over 60+ subjects with extensive study materials from the best faculty members of Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology. And guess what, learning is no more a headache.

Our goal is to improve the learning experience of every student and help them succeed in their education. We believe that different perspectives lead to better ideas. So the hands on notes from best and most trusted teachers will give you a clear perspective and will definitely help you understand the subjects better. This will surely make you fall in love with them. We make learning Fun!
